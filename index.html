Osman Keshawarz
Assistant Professor of Labor Economics
University of Hawaiʻi–West Oʻahu

I am a labor economist specializing in political economy, public policy, and the dynamics of housing and labor markets. My research leverages empirical microeconomics and causal inference to understand how policy shocks shape inequality.

Beyond academia, I apply econometric modeling, data science, and advanced statistical analysis to consulting projects, helping organizations make sense of complex economic data.
